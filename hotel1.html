Hotels for Ukrainians.

1) Premier Inn

Premier Inn is a popular hotel company that has willingly noticed The Ukrainians situation and has provided them with their service. A quote from their website,"We’ve all seen the humanitarian crisis unfolding in Ukraine in recent weeks. Our hearts are with all those whose lives have been affected by these devastating events, and the unimaginable suffering caused by this needless human tragedy. The Whitbread family of hotels and restaurants, including Premier Inn, are fortunate enough to employ people from all over the world and our immediate priority has been supporting our colleagues to ensure they have the help they need in these incredibly difficult circumstances. In further response to the crisis and as part of our Force for Good strategy, we’ve announced that we will be pledging a minimum of £500,000 to support the charities of The Disasters Emergency Committee (DEC) and their international humanitarian aid effort."
